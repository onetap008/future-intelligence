Robotics is the field of engineering that focuses on designing, building, and using robots. Robots are machines that can perform tasks automatically or with minimal human help.

Robots are used in factories to assemble cars, in hospitals for surgeries, and even in space missions. They help make difficult, dangerous, or repetitive tasks easier and more efficient.

Robotics combines many fields like mechanical engineering, electronics, and computer science.
